This example contains a pattern known as a glider, on a 10x5 toroidal board.

The pattern is a type of spaceship - a pattern that travels across the board, eventually reappearing in a different position while maintaining its orientation.

The glider is the smallest spaceship in the Game of Life, and was originally discovered in 1969 by mathematician Richard K. Guy.
