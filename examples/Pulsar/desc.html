This example contains a pattern known as a Pulsar, on a 17x17 non-toroidal board.

The pattern is a type of oscillator - a pattern that is a predecessor of itself.

The pulsar was found in March 1970 by Game of Life creator John Conway.

Even though it is large, it is one of the most common oscillators.
