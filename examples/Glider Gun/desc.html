This example contains a pattern known as a Gosper glider gun, on a 40x15 toroidal board.

The pattern is a type of gun - a pattern that emits spaceships at regular intervals.

The Gosper glider gun was the first gun discovered in the Game of Life, in 1970 by mathematician Bill Gosper.
